OUR MISSION


Western Union County Organization Spotlight is an website that puts a spotlight (hence the name) on organizations in the western part of Union County, NJ. During the COVID-19 pandemic, there has been a boom in organizations (especially student led ones). However, these organizations have a tough time finding benefactors or volunteers because they don't have a platform to advertise themselves on. That's where we come in. Our site provides a free platform for these new organizations to advertise themselves on, allowing them to grow and flourish.

ORGANIZATION OF THE WEEK



Organization of the Week: Little Free Library of New Providence

Western Union County Organization Spotlight Team, August 28, 2020

From Little Free Library of New Providence: We have put a Little Free Library in the Strawberry Fields in town. A little Free Library is a place where people can donate or take books for free. It is kind of like a mini town library. Since our town library is closed, it is a way for people to access free books. If you want to find out more about Little Free Library you can look here: https://littlefreelibrary.org/.
