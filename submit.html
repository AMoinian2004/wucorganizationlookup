Submit Your Organization To Be Featured On Our Website


GUIDELINES FOR SUBMISSION


  • Must be based in one of the following townships: New Providence, Summit, Berkley Heights, Scotch Plains/Fanwood, Clark, Springfield, Westfield, Mountainside.
  • Must be a non-profit, freelance/online bussiness, or student led organization. It cannot be a bussiness that has a storefront.
  • No political organizations.

STEPS FOR SUBMISSION


  1. Include the name of your non profit.
  2. Discuss its mission, in a couple sentenses.
  3. Include the Website (if you have one) and social media links (if you have social media).
