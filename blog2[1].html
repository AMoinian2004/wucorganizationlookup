Organization of the Week: Litte Free Library of New Providence

Western Union County Organization Spotlight Team, August 28, 2020


From Litte Free Library of New Providence: We have put a Little Free Library in the Strawberry Fields in town. A little Free Library is a place where people can donate or take books for free. It is kind of like a mini town library. Since our town library is closed, it is a way for people to access free books. If you want to find out more about Little Free Library you can look here: https://littlefreelibrary.org/.
